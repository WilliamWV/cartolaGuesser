CartolaGuesser Game:

	* One sentence def: Jogador busca fazer mais pontos do que as escalaçãos dos BOTS
	* Features:
		- Central:
			1) BOT's squad can only be seen after the closing of the market
			2) 19 different BOTs are used, the BOTs vary on AI prediction model
			3) The player will join in a championship against the BOTS where he and the BOTs will form squads throughout the real league and score according to Cartola's score
			
		- Additionals:
			101) Conquest system
			102) The player could be able to consider or not a league round score before it happens in real life, but this decision must not be able to be changed after the market is closed

		A.101) Conquests:
			
			Conquests rewards:
				* EASY: 10 xp
				* MID: 50 xp
				* HARD: 300xp
			
			Conquest list:
				* MVP: Be the top scorer of one round - MID (amoung the BOTS) : Every round
				* LVP: Be the bottom scorer of one round - MID (amoung the BOTS) : Every round
				* CAMPEÃO: Be the top scorer of the entire championship - HARD (amoung the BOTS) : Once the league
				* OK: Beat the average of Cartola's scores in one round - EASY : Every round
				* MITO HOJE: Have the top scorer football player on your squad in one round- EASY : Every round
				* MITO SEMPRE: Have the top scorer football player on your squad in at least half of the championship rounds - MID : Once the league
				* RETRANCA: Have none of your defenders suffering a goal in one round - EASY : Every round
				* JOGAMOS COMO NUNCA PERDEMOS COMO SEMPRE: beat your own score of a round but still don't be the MVP of the round - MID : Every round
				* PEDE MÚSICA: Have one of your players scoring at least three goals on the same match - MID : Every round
				* 7x1: Have your players scoring a total of 7 goals and taking a total of 1 goal - MID: Every round
